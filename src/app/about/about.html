Meet C-Squad Photography – Capturing Life Through a Bold, Feminine Lens

C-Squad Photography is a dynamic trio of talented female photographers united by a shared passion for storytelling through imagery. With diverse backgrounds in portrait, event, and artistic photography, Jade Carter, Naomi Cruz, and Priya Carlson bring creativity, precision, and a fresh perspective to every project.

Jade thrives in dynamic environments, whether it’s a high-fashion editorial or an urban street shoot. Naomi specializes in raw, candid moments—think tearful vows at weddings or unguarded laughter between loved ones.
And Priya brings an architectural eye to every shot, transforming ordinary scenes into striking visual narratives. Together, they’re C-Squad — a dream team that balances technical precision with heart. Whether it’s an intimate wedding, a high-energy brand shoot, or a striking editorial, C-Squad blends technical expertise with an intuitive eye for emotion and composition. Their collaborative approach ensures each client’s vision is brought to life with authenticity and flair.
